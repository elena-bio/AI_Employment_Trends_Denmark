# Navigating the AI Era: Employment Trends and Future Outlook in Denmark

A data-driven analysis exploring the integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and the evolution of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) skills across private industries in Denmark between 2021 and 2025.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement & Research Questions
As AI adoption increases, understanding its impact on employment and workforce skills has become increasingly important in Denmark. This project focuses on answering four key questions:
1. How has AI adoption changed over time in Denmark?
2. How have ICT skills evolved over time?
3. Which industries show the highest levels of AI adoption and ICT skills?
4. What do these trends suggest about the future workforce in Denmark?

---

## 📊 Key Insights & Findings
* **Steady Growth:** Both AI adoption and ICT skills have shown a clear upward trajectory, indicating growing digital readiness.
* **Industry Leaders vs. Laggards:** The Information and Communication sector heavily leads both in AI adoption and ICT skills. Conversely, the Construction sector displays the lowest levels of digital integration.
* **Workforce Impact:** There is an expanding demand for technology-driven occupations, creating potential skill gaps across less digitized industries. Continuous learning and upskilling are vital.
